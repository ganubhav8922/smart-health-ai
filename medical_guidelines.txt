=== HEATSTROKE PRECAUTIONS ===
When ambient temperatures exceed 38 degrees Celsius, individuals should limit outdoor activities between 11 AM and 4 PM. Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily. Symptoms of heat exhaustion include heavy sweating, rapid pulse, and dizziness. If a person stops sweating and becomes confused, it is a medical emergency (heatstroke).

=== DENGUE FEVER GUIDELINES ===
Dengue virus is transmitted by female Aedes mosquitoes. Prevention requires eliminating standing water in buckets, coolers, and flower pots once a week. Symptoms include high fever, severe headache, pain behind the eyes, and joint pain. Avoid taking Aspirin or Ibuprofen for suspected dengue as it can worsen bleeding; use Paracetamol instead.
